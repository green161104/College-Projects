What is Reclica Textil?

Reclica Textil is a non-profit organization dedicated to reducing textile waste and supporting communities in need by collecting, recycling, and redistributing clothing and other textiles.

What types of items can I donate?

We accept all types of clothing, shoes, accessories, and household textiles. Items should be clean and in good condition.

How can I volunteer with Reclica Textil?

We welcome volunteers for various roles including sorting donations, assisting at events, and community outreach. Please fill out the volunteer application form on our website to get started.

How does Reclica Textil ensure that donations are used responsibly?

We work with a network of trusted partners and conduct regular audits to ensure that donations are distributed responsibly and transparently.
