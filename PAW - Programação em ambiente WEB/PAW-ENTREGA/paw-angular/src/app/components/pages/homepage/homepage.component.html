Our Purpose

At ReciclaTextil, we're driven by a powerful mission: to make a transformative impact on communities through the collection and redistribution of gently used clothing. With a heartfelt dedication to sustainability and social responsibility, we channel our efforts into providing essential clothing items to various institutions, including shelters, orphanages, and community centers. Every donation we receive represents a beacon of hope for individuals and families in need, empowering them with dignity and warmth. With your invaluable support, we can expand our reach, touch more lives, and foster a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
